knots_to_mph¶

tropycal.utils.knots_to_mph(wind_speed)[source]¶

Convert wind from knots to miles per hour, in increments of 5 as used by NHC.

Parameters

wind_speed (int) – Sustained wind in knots.

Returns

int – Sustained wind in miles per hour.
